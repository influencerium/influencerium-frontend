# Influencerium - Design System & Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Brand Name
**Influencerium** - The Premier Platform for Creator-Brand Collaboration

### Brand Tagline
"Where Influence Meets Opportunity"

### Brand Mission
Empower creators to monetize their influence and help brands reach authentic audiences through meaningful collaborations.

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Purple:** `#7C3AED` (Vibrant, creative, premium)
- **Primary Accent:** `#EC4899` (Energy, engagement, growth)

### Secondary Colors
- **Dark Background:** `#0F172A` (Deep navy, professional)
- **Light Background:** `#F8FAFC` (Clean, modern)
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` (Clarity, trust)

### Accent Colors
- **Success Green:** `#10B981` (Positive actions, growth)
- **Warning Orange:** `#F59E0B` (Alerts, important info)
- **Error Red:** `#EF4444` (Errors, critical)
- **Info Blue:** `#3B82F6` (Information, help)

### Neutral Colors
- **Text Dark:** `#1E293B` (Primary text)
- **Text Light:** `#64748B` (Secondary text)
- **Border:** `#E2E8F0` (Subtle borders)
- **Hover:** `#F1F5F9` (Hover states)

---

## Typography

### Font Family
- **Primary Font:** Inter (Modern, clean, professional)
- **Heading Font:** Poppins (Bold, distinctive, creative)
- **Monospace:** Fira Code (Code, technical content)

### Font Sizes
- **H1 (Hero):** 48px, Bold (700)
- **H2 (Section):** 36px, Bold (700)
- **H3 (Subsection):** 28px, Semi-Bold (600)
- **H4 (Card Title):** 20px, Semi-Bold (600)
- **Body Large:** 18px, Regular (400)
- **Body Regular:** 16px, Regular (400)
- **Body Small:** 14px, Regular (400)
- **Caption:** 12px, Regular (400)

---

## Logo Design

### Logo Concept
A stylized upward arrow combined with a star, representing growth and influence.

### Logo Variations
1. **Full Logo:** Icon + Text (Influencerium)
2. **Icon Only:** Standalone mark for favicons, avatars
3. **Horizontal:** Logo with text on the right
4. **Vertical:** Logo with text below

### Logo Colors
- **Primary:** Purple (#7C3AED)
- **Accent:** Pink (#EC4899)
- **Monochrome:** White or Dark

---

## Component Design

### Buttons
- **Primary Button:** Purple background, white text, rounded corners (8px)
- **Secondary Button:** White background, purple text, purple border
- **Tertiary Button:** Transparent, purple text
- **Disabled:** Gray background, gray text

### Cards
- **Background:** White
- **Border:** Subtle gray border (1px)
- **Shadow:** Soft shadow on hover
- **Padding:** 24px
- **Border Radius:** 12px

### Input Fields
- **Background:** Light gray (#F8FAFC)
- **Border:** 1px solid #E2E8F0
- **Focus:** Purple border, subtle shadow
- **Padding:** 12px 16px
- **Border Radius:** 8px

### Navigation
- **Background:** White with subtle shadow
- **Text:** Dark gray
- **Active:** Purple text with underline
- **Hover:** Light purple background

## Accessibility

### Color Contrast
- Minimum WCAG AA compliance (4.5:1 for text)
- WCAG AAA compliance where possible (7:1)

### Typography
- Minimum font size: 14px for body text
- Line height: 1.5 for readability
- Letter spacing: 0.5px for headings

### Interactive Elements
- Minimum touch target: 44px × 44px
- Clear focus states
- Keyboard navigation support

---

## Usage Guidelines

### Do's
✅ Use the primary purple for main CTAs
✅ Use the accent pink for highlights and secondary elements
✅ Maintain consistent spacing and alignment
✅ Use the brand fonts for all text
✅ Keep the logo clear and uncluttered

### Don'ts
❌ Don't change the logo colors without approval
❌ Don't use other color combinations
❌ Don't distort or rotate the logo
❌ Don't add effects or shadows to the logo
❌ Don't use the logo on busy backgrounds
